How Lunara Works

Simple, secure, and transparent process in four easy steps.

1

Create Project

Define scope, milestones, and payment terms. Both parties agree to the terms.

2

Fund Escrow

Client deposits project payment into secure escrow. Funds are held safely until work is completed.

3

Complete Work

Freelancer delivers milestone work. Client reviews and approves deliverables through our platform.

4

Release Payment

Upon approval, payment is automatically released to the freelancer. Milestone completed successfully.
