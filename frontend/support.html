Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions.

How do I start a project?

Go to “Get Started” on the main page, create a project, and define your milestones.

Is Lunara free?

Yes! Both freelancers and clients can use Lunara for free. Payments only happen when milestones are met.

What if there’s a dispute?

Our mediation team steps in quickly to resolve conflicts fairly for both parties.
